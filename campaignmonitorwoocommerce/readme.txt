== Installation ==
**To connect WooCommerce store to Campaign Monitor:**

* Log in to your WordPress dashboard and click Plugins in the left sidebar.
* Click Add New at the top of the page then search for “Campaign Monitor for WooCommerce”.
* In search results, click the Install Now button on the “Campaign Monitor for WooCommerce” plugin.
* Click Activate Plugin.
* In the left sidebar, click Campaign Monitor for WcoCommerce.
* Click on connect and follow the instructions to connect Campaign Monitor to your WooCommerce Store.

What is Campaign Monitor?
Campaign Monitor makes it radically easy to create, send and measure the impact of your email marketing campaigns.


This plugin allows you to import customer data from your WooCommerce store into your Campaign Monitor lists so you can send beautifully designed, personalized emails to your customers. Don’t have a Campaign Monitor account? Sign up for free.
